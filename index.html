How to use
  • Vertices are static once placed (they do not move).
  • Add vertex: choose Add Vertex and click on canvas.
  • Add edge: choose Add Edge, click two vertices.
  • Select edge: click an edge (Shift+click to multi-select non-interfering flippable edges).
  • Flip: select edges and press Flip Selected or press F.
  • Delete: choose Delete and click a vertex or edge to remove.
Flippable edge: must be shared by exactly two triangles (two distinct opposite vertices). Parallel flips cannot share a triangle, and their new edges must not conflict (no duplicate new edges or new edges that become two edges of the same triangle).
